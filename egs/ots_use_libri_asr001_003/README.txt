
 The ots USE_ASR001  corpus is a 120+ hour corpus of English read speech
 derived from content specified in the its original project, sampled at 48kHz, 2-channel.  The
 accents are various and labeled,they are US English.  It is not publicly released.  It was prepared
 as a speech recognition corpus by Chi Zhang using librispeech as template.

 The recipe is in s5/
